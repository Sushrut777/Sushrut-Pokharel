Career goal

A career summary is brief, general statement on a resume that includes overview of experience, responsibilities of a job, skills, and accomplishments.
The career summary is the first item on the resume, after the contact information and serves as an introduction to the rest of the resume.
Career summary is often targeted to the job in which the applicant is interested. The main goal of a career summary is to share the most immpresive qualifications relevant to the job up front and grab the hiring manager's attention with it.
